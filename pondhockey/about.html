About the Pond League

2025–2026 Season

The Pond League is designed for players who want a slow‑paced, fun‑focused experience with minimal competitiveness. It’s an inclusive, recreational environment where beginners, seniors, mentors, and C‑level or below skaters can enjoy hockey in a safe and social atmosphere.

League spirit

This league is FOR FUN! A low level of play is required regardless of ability. Players are expected to respect the recreational intent of the league — focusing on camaraderie, safety, and enjoyment above all else.
