FAQ

This is a strictly non‑competitive recreational co‑ed league with players of all abilities. The Pond League is intentionally low‑key. It’s great for beginners, seniors, mentors, and C‑level or below players. Highly skilled players can be an asset to the league as mentors and are free to join provided they embrace the character of the League and facilitate learning the game for teammates and opponents. The goal is fun, safety, and development of team chemistry. Captains and referees will help uphold this playing standard
Team registration is closed for this season. We are maxed out at 8 teams. Free agents can also be placed on existing teams if required by the commissioner.
